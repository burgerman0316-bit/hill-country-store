Welcome to Hill's

Proudly serving the Elburn community, Hill's Country Store is your classic, reliable neighborhood stop. Stop on by for friendly conversation, quick snacks, everyday essentials, and specialty local items. Look out for our signature vibrant purple storefront on Harter Road!

What We Offer

Cold Drinks

We stock a massive selection of ice-cold sodas, energy drinks, premium sports drinks, bottled waters, and local juices kept chilled to perfection.

Fresh Breakfast

Try our locally famous, hot homemade breakfast burritos and fresh morning pastries. The ultimate grab-and-go start to your day!

Live Bait

Heading out to the water? Yes, we really do stock seasonal live bait! Check the yellow window sign or ask our staff at the main counter for today's current stock.

Convenience Items

From chips, candy bars, and bags of ice to essential household products, automotive fluids, and travel toiletries—we have your quick needs covered.
