Welcome to Hill's

Proudly serving the Kaneville community, Hill's Country Store is your classic, reliable neighborhood stop. Stop on by for friendly conversation, quick snacks, everyday essentials, and specialty local items. Look out for our signature vibrant purple storefront on Harter Road!

What We Offer

Homemade Breakfast

Try our signature homemade breakfast burritos made hot and fresh to start your morning right, alongside a selection of morning pastries and breakfast wraps.

Ice Cream Treats

Cool down with our selection of ice cream novelties, classic popsicles, and premium frozen treats perfect for hot summer days.

Cold Drinks

We stock a massive selection of ice-cold sodas, energy drinks, premium sports drinks, bottled waters, and local juices kept chilled to perfection.

Snack & Candy

Pick up all your favorite brand-name potato chips, pretzels, chocolate candy bars, gummies, nuts, and quick bite provisions.

Live Bait

Heading out to the water? Yes, we really do stock seasonal live bait! Check the yellow window sign or ask our staff at the main counter for today's current stock.

Daily Essentials

Save yourself a long trip to the supermarket. We carry bags of ice, basic household staples, pantry goods, and travel-sized commodities.
